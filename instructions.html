How to Play

The Goal

You need to recreate the target shape by combining different rotating circles. Each circle has three properties:

  • Radius: How big the circle's orbit is
  • Frequency: How fast and in which direction it rotates
  • Phase: Where it starts in its rotation

Understanding the Target

This is the target shape you need to recreate. It's an ellipse formed by combining two circles in a chain:

Circle 1: Large green circle
Starts at the center and creates the main elliptical path
Circle 2: Small yellow circle
Rotates around Circle 1's position, creating the final shape

The Gameplay

1. Choose circles:You'll see animated previews of each circle's motion

2. Drag and drop: Place the correct circles in the slots

3. Check your solution: Click "Check Solution" to see how your circles combine

4. Get feedback: You'll see how many circles are correct

Mastermind-Style Feedback

Use this feedback to figure out which circles are correct and which are not!

Tip: Start by looking at the target shape. Is it round? Pointy? How many loops does it have? This will help you guess which circles to use!
